Story behind the a-mood name

It is supposedly said that a-mood was born from the idealist thought of the idea initiator, who is also its business owner, then.
She loves bohemian, ethnic and gothic fashion but she is very moody in designing. Her fully name is Sastranaling, and Aling is her nickname. One day, she had an idea to creat a brand name called a-mood, which initial (a) is representative of Aling. So, in a shortly meaning, a-mood means the Aling's mood. In depth philosophy, if we read a-mood in English pronounsation, it would mean REMEMBER. We hope that more people will remember us and our collections as their fashion reference. In additional, if its literally read, a-mood means ACCOMODATE. We accomodate fashion for all ages, body shapes, young and old of women in particular.
